Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence ("ANCRE Cardiac Coherence", "we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the ANCRE Cardiac Coherence application.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to ANCRE Cardiac Coherence, we collect:

  • TikTok Account Information: Your TikTok username and account identifier, solely for authentication and content publishing purposes.
  • OAuth Tokens: Authentication tokens provided by TikTok to authorize content posting on your behalf.

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence does not collect:

  • Personal messages or direct messages
  • Follower lists or following lists
  • Video viewing history or analytics
  • Payment or financial information
  • Location data

3. How We Use Your Information

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence uses the collected information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account connection
  • Upload and publish cardiac coherence breathing exercise videos to your TikTok account
  • Schedule video content for future publication

4. Data Storage and Security

Your authentication data is stored securely on our self-hosted server infrastructure. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to TikTok as required for content publishing through their official API.

6. Data Retention

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence retains your account information only for as long as your TikTok account is connected to the App. You may request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting your account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your TikTok account from ANCRE Cardiac Coherence at any time
  • Request deletion of all stored data
  • Revoke app permissions through TikTok account settings

8. Children's Privacy

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
