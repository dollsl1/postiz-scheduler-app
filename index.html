ANCRE Cardiac Coherence

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence is an application that schedules and publishes cardiac coherence breathing exercise videos on TikTok.

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence provides guided breathing exercises to help users practice cardiac coherence for stress reduction and well-being.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

About

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish educational breathing exercise videos. The app posts pre-generated video content on a scheduled basis to provide daily cardiac coherence exercises to followers.
