ANCRE - Cardiac Coherence Scheduler

This application helps schedule and publish cardiac coherence breathing exercise videos on TikTok.

ANCRE provides guided breathing exercises to help users practice cardiac coherence for stress reduction and well-being.

About

ANCRE Cardiac Coherence uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish educational breathing exercise videos. The app posts pre-generated video content on a scheduled basis to provide daily cardiac coherence exercises to followers.
